What's Our Story

It was late 2009, and working with outsourcing companies in India, Ukraine, Sri Lanka, and Poland had become a frustrating experience for a select few. Three persistent difficulties come up time and time again, and these are the issues of code quality, communication efficiency, and commitment level. The notion was that if we could address these issues and deliver specialist solutions to the corporate sector, we’d be in good shape. We chose Functional Programming as our vehicle. After over two decades of using Java, Functional Programming was an intriguing option for developing the next generation of business solutions.
